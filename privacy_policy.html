Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the Plant Watering Reminder, Growth Tracker, and Plant Care App.

1. Information You Provide

We do not collect personal data. Any information you enter—such as plant names, watering schedules, care notes, or growth logs—remains on your device and, if enabled, in your iCloud storage.

2. Cloud Backup and Data Storage

If you activate cloud backup, your plant-related data is securely stored in your private iCloud account managed by Apple CloudKit. We cannot view, edit, or access this data at any time.

3. Notifications and Reminders

The app sends custom reminders only after you grant permission. Notification data stays on your device and is never transmitted externally.

4. Subscription & Payment Information

All payments, subscriptions, and billing information are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not process or store any financial or personal payment data.

5. Analytics & Tracking

This app does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not monitor usage behavior.

6. Data Security

Your data benefits from Apple’s robust system-level security. Plant data stored in iCloud is encrypted and protected by your Apple ID.

7. Your Control Over Data

You may delete plant data at any time through the app. You may also disable cloud backup or notifications whenever you choose.

8. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to clarify practices or comply with legal requirements. Continued use means you accept any changes.
